Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our resort and services

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Standard check-in time is 12:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

How far is Paradise Resort from India Gate?

Paradise Resort is located just a few minutes away from India Gate, offering easy access to central Delhi.

Is free Wi-Fi available in the rooms and public areas?

Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi is complimentary throughout the property.

Is there a minimum stay requirement?

During peak seasons and holidays, there may be a minimum stay requirement of 2 nights. Please check availability for your desired dates.
