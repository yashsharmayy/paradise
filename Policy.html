Privacy Policy

At Paradise Resort Delhi, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit or use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, payment details, and identification (if required).
  • Booking Information: Hotel name, dates of stay, number of guests, preferences, and special requests.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage data through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process bookings and payments
  • To send booking confirmations and customer service communications
  • To personalize user experience and improve our website
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share it with:

  • Hotels or accommodations you book
  • Payment processors
  • Legal authorities (if required)

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security protocols (SSL, encryption, access controls) to protect your data.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and for marketing purposes. You may control cookie usage through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at paradiseresortdelhi@gmail.com.

Return and Refund Policy

At Paradise Resort Delhi, we strive to provide a smooth and transparent booking experience. Please read our return and refund policy carefully.

1. Cancellation Policy

  • Refundable Bookings: Cancellations made before the free cancellation deadline (as stated on your booking confirmation) are eligible for a full refund.
  • Non-refundable Bookings: These bookings are not eligible for a refund under any circumstances.

2. No-Shows

If you do not check-in on the booked date and haven’t cancelled, the entire booking amount may be forfeited as per the hotel’s policy.

3. Refund Process

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days after approval.
  • Transaction charges (if any) are non-refundable.

4. Modifications

Changes to the booking are subject to hotel availability and may incur additional charges. Please contact our support team for assistance.

5. Disputes or Issues

For complaints, discrepancies, or dispute resolution, contact us at paradiseresortdelhi@gmail.com with your booking reference number.
