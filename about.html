About Paradise Resort Delhi

Our Story

In the heart of the capital, near the iconic India Gate, Paradise Resort Delhi was founded to redefine luxury hospitality. Inspired by the vision of providing comfort, elegance, and world-class service, the resort has become a symbol of relaxation and sophistication.

Since its foundation, Paradise Resort has been serving travelers from across the world, offering modern amenities with a touch of traditional Indian warmth. We take pride in blending cultural heritage with contemporary luxury for an unforgettable experience.

With state-of-the-art facilities, spacious rooms, and exceptional service, Paradise Resort Delhi welcomes guests to experience comfort and peace in the heart of the bustling city.
